What HereNow Is

HereNow is a location-aware social layer for the real world. Not a dating app. Not a feed app. An IRL presence layer that answers one question: who's here right now, and why?

Users check into venues (zones), set their Social Mode and Mood, see who else is there in real time, and connect IRL via the We Met handshake — which unlocks a 72-hour DM window. Venues can follow along in a live dashboard, post promotions to their followers, and send announcements directly to anyone who's subscribed.

Core Concepts

📍
Zone
A venue — bar, coffee shop, gym, event space — with a GPS center and a geofence radius. Users enter a zone when they step inside the radius.
🎭
Social Mode
Why you're out: Dating / Friends / Networking / Just Vibes. Set at check-in. Visible to others in the zone so intent is clear from the start.
🟢
Mood Mode
How approachable you are: Open (come say hi) / Selective (quality over quantity) / Not Today (hard boundary — no approach). Shown on your People card.
🤝
We Met
Mutual IRL confirmation. Both people tap "We Met" on each other's card. DMs stay LOCKED while both are at the venue. When you check out, the 72-hour DM window opens. Pending requests expire 4 hours after your session ends.
💌
Direct Messages
72-hour window, unlocked on checkout. The time pressure is intentional — it mirrors the natural social context of having just met someone. Premium tier can extend this (planned).
📡
Pulse
Ephemeral in-venue social feed. Posts visible only to people checked in at the same zone, right now. Posts expire in 12 hours. A moment, not a record.
💬
Venue Chat
Real-time group chat for everyone currently checked in. 24-hour expiry. No archive, no screenshots encouraged — what happens at the venue stays at the venue.
✨
Afterglow
Auto-generated session recap when you check out. Duration, people count, connections made, badges earned.

V2+ Roadmap

Premium Venue Tiers

Analytics dashboard, branded check-in pages, promoted events, priority placement in discovery feed. Stripe integration for venue subscriptions.

Group We Met

Multiple people in a group connect at once — for when you meet a table, not just one person.

Ghost Mode

Invisible to the People list but still able to see others. For users who want to observe before engaging.

Venue Claiming

Non-owner venues (seeded from maps data) can be claimed by real owners with verification step.

Pulse Templates

Suggested post prompts based on venue type — "How's the service tonight?" at a restaurant, "What are you drinking?" at a bar.

Extended DM Windows

Premium users get longer DM windows (7 days, 14 days). Incentivizes upgrade while keeping free tier experiential.

Regular User — Step by Step

1
Sign up
Email + password + display name + username. Select "Person" toggle. Email confirmation required.
2
Build profile
Age range, up to 16 interest tags (Music, Gaming, Art, etc.), conversation kickoff ("ask me about..."), optional avatar. Check-in privacy toggle: Full or Minimal.
3
Open the map
Nearby tab shows venues on a dark map with geofence circles and live check-in counts. Feed tab shows discovery posts and followed-venue updates.
4
Arrive at a venue
Zone page: who's here, Pulse feed, Chat, Events. + Follow button to subscribe to venue promos. Venue highlights pinned at top.
5
Check in
Choose Social Mode (why you're here) + Mood Mode (how approachable). Appears in People list for others. Privacy setting controls what's visible.
6
Connect
Tap someone's card → view profile → tap We Met. They get a push notification. Both confirm → DMs locked until checkout.
7
Check out → Afterglow
72-hour DM window opens. Afterglow shows duration, people present, connections made, badges earned.

Venue Owner — Step by Step

1
Sign up as a venue
Toggle to "Venue" on signup. Enter venue name, type (Bar / Coffee Shop / Restaurant / Event Space / etc.), email, password. Profile created with venue_status = pending.
2
Pending review
Dashboard shows "Under Review" state. Admin approves via admin panel. Usually within 24-48 hours.
3
Set venue location
Edit Venue → "Use My Location" → choose radius (Small 80m / Medium 150m / Large 300m) → Save. Geofencing goes live immediately. No manual lat/lng needed.
4
Dashboard goes live
Live check-in counter, subscriber count, age range bars, interest tag cloud. Quick actions to all tools.
5
Market to followers
Promotions: title + discount label + description + optional universal feed post. Announcements: 280-char direct to subscribers. Both appear in followers' feed instantly.
6
Manage appearance
Highlights: pin up to 5 items on zone page. Events: create trivia nights, DJ sets, happy hours. All visible to users on the zone's Events tab.

Questions for Jacob — Shaping the Vision

These questions are here to make sure every decision we make builds toward what you actually need. No wrong answers — the more detail, the better the product.

  • 1When you picture a perfect night at your venue using HereNow — what does it look like? Walk us through it from the moment someone walks in.
  • 2What's the main thing you want from a venue owner perspective — more foot traffic, better customer relationships, or something else?
  • 3When you send an announcement to followers, what would a real example look like? (e.g. "DJ starts at 10, free entry before 9" — or something totally different?)
  • 4How often would you realistically send a promotion or announcement? Daily? Weekly? Only for special events?
  • 5Right now users have to manually tap Follow on a venue. Would you prefer they get auto-subscribed when they check in, or should it stay opt-in?
  • 6How do you feel about users seeing each other's age range and interests at your venue? Is that a feature or a concern for your crowd?
  • 7The DM window closes 72 hours after checkout. Does that feel right to you, or would you want it longer or shorter?
  • 8What types of venues do you see HereNow being most powerful in — bars, restaurants, gyms, events, co-working spaces, something else?
  • 9Is there a type of venue where you think HereNow would NOT work, and why?
  • 10How important is the "anonymous" or privacy angle to users at your venue? Do people want to be seen, or do they want control?
  • 11What would make you recommend HereNow to another venue owner? What's the "aha" moment you'd tell them about?
  • 12If you could add ONE thing to the venue dashboard that isn't there yet, what would it be?
  • 13Would you want to be able to respond to or interact with users' Pulse posts as the venue? (e.g. pin a post, react to it, reply officially)
  • 14How do you feel about the venue chat — do you want to be able to jump in as the venue, or should it stay user-only?
  • 15On the user side — would you (or people you know) actually use the We Met feature? Does the concept of in-person confirmation before DMs feel right?
  • 16Are there safety or moderation concerns you're thinking about as a venue owner? What would make you feel confident running this at your spot?
  • 17Would you want a QR code at the door that sends people straight to check-in? Or does the GPS auto-detection feel like enough?
  • 18Do you see HereNow as something your regulars use, new visitors, or both?
  • 19If we built a premium venue tier with deeper analytics and featured placement — what would it be worth to you per month?
  • 20What would have to be true for HereNow to be a tool you open every single night you're running the venue?
